Tools for Building Footings

The following tools will be needed for construction of footings;
  1. Tape Measure
  2. Pencil
  3. Builders Level
  4. Surveyors Level or Laser Level
  5. Excavator
  6. Wheel Barrow
  7. String Line
  8. Safety Glass
  9. Shovel
  10. Hose
  11. Engineers Plans
NOTE: The construction of piers, pads & footings should all be engineered for the individual purpose that they serve. The preparation work should always be checked by the certifying authority before concrete is poured into footings.
