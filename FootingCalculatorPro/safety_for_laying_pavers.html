Safety for Building Footings

When constructing footings, keeping a clean and tidy work site at all times is not only good practice but also ensures safety. These are some safety tips for construction;
  1. Check all your equipment.
  2. Keep construction site clean & tidy at all times.
  3. Ensure that you read the operating manuals of all equipment including hired equipment such as excavators, vibrators, etc.
  4. Check the condition of the hand tools like hammers, chisels, and screw drivers, to make sure that they are in safe working order before use.
  5. Always operate equipment according to the manufactures instructions and conditions.
  6. All power tools should be operated through a safety switch board.
  7. Wear eye & ear protection at all times operating heavy machinery.
  8. Wear protective clothing, safety shoes, etc. at all times.
  9. Locate local services within construction site before digging or excavating.
  10. Consult with the local authority about rules & regulations for construction guidelines in your local area for the job that you are performing.
NOTE: Ensure all footings are designed to detailed certified engineers drawings.
